# VKAD Design & UI Doctrine
**Status:** Canonical Design Blueprint
**Last Updated:** January 2026

## 0. The Golden Rule
This project is **READING-FIRST**. The Reading Page is the **PRIMARY OBJECT** of design. All other pages (homepage, menus, navigation) are secondary and must structurally support the Reading Page.

## 1. Visual Style & Atmosphere
- **Aesthetic:** Serene, book-like, scholarly, and timeless.
- **Palette:** Off-white/parchment backgrounds (`#F9F7F2`), soft contrast, no saturation.
- **Typography:** Elegant serif for scripture reading; restrained sans-serif for UI metadata.

## 2. Mega Menu Architecture
The Mega Menu must be spacious and breathable.

### A. READ (Canonical Hierarchy)
- **Column 1:** Fixed Śāstra categories (Śruti, Smṛti, etc.).
- **Column 2:** Dynamic sub-texts based on Column 1 hover.
- **Column 3:** Pedagogical guides (How to Read, Recommended Order).
- **Column 4:** Continuity (Continue Reading, Saved) - Hidden from guests.

### B. EXPLORE THEMES
- **Format:** Visual grid of icon + title blocks.
- **Rule:** Do not display scripture names at this level to maintain visual silence.

### C. LIFE PATHS
- **Personas:** Parents, Youth, Newly Married, Seekers, Practitioners, Researchers.

## 3. Interaction & Silence
- **Auto-Hide:** Global Header must hide on scroll during reading.
- **Search:** Full-screen overlay; no suggestions before intent.
- **Panels:** Left (Navigation) and Right (Context/Commentary) are collapsed by default.
- **Zen Mode:** No popups, no ads, no infinite scroll.
