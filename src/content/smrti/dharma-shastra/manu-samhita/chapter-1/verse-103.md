viduṣā brāhmaṇenedaṁ adhyetavyaṁ prayatnataḥ |
śiṣyebhyaśca pravaktavyaṁ samyan nānyena kenacit ||

---

**Translation:**
A learned Brahmana must carefully study this whole system, and he must duly instruct his pupils in it, but nobody else shall do it.
